# Banking Module – End-to-End Integration, Ledger Relationship & Workflow Enhancement

The current **Banking** module is not fully integrated with the rest of the ERP system. Banking transactions, ledgers, accounting entries, sales, purchases, expenses, and other financial modules are operating independently instead of following a complete accounting workflow. This needs to be redesigned and implemented as a fully integrated, enterprise-grade banking and finance module.

## Banking Integration

Implement a complete end-to-end banking workflow where every financial transaction is properly linked to the corresponding business process.

The Banking module must be fully integrated with:

* General Ledger (GL)
* Chart of Accounts
* Sales
* Purchase
* Accounts Receivable
* Accounts Payable
* Expenses
* Payments
* Receipts
* Cash Management
* Inventory Valuation
* Tax Management
* Customer Accounts
* Vendor Accounts
* Journal Entries
* Financial Reports
* Reconciliation
* Audit Logs

Every financial transaction should automatically generate the appropriate accounting entries without requiring manual intervention.

---

## Ledger Integration

Currently, the Ledger is not properly connected with the application's financial modules.

Implement a complete ledger relationship where:

* Every Sales Invoice posts automatically to the General Ledger.
* Every Purchase Invoice creates accounting entries automatically.
* Expense transactions post to the correct expense accounts.
* Customer payments update Accounts Receivable and Bank Accounts.
* Vendor payments update Accounts Payable and Bank Accounts.
* Journal Entries immediately update the Ledger.
* Bank Transfers update both source and destination accounts.
* Refunds, Returns, Credit Notes, Debit Notes, and Adjustments generate proper accounting entries.
* All ledger balances remain synchronized in real time.

No transaction should exist without corresponding ledger entries.

* Debits and credits remain balanced.
* Account balances update correctly.

## Validation & Testing

After implementation, verify that:

* Every banking transaction updates the General Ledger.
* Sales, Purchases, Expenses, Receipts, and Payments are correctly reflected in financial records.
* Bank balances remain accurate.
* Trial Balance and financial statements reconcile correctly.
* Relationships between all financial modules are maintained.
* No existing business logic, workflows, APIs, validations, or database operations are broken.

## Final Deliverable

Transform the Banking module into a fully integrated, enterprise-grade financial management system where Banking, Accounting, Sales, Purchases, Expenses, and the General Ledger operate seamlessly together. The solution should provide complete transaction traceability, automated accounting entries, robust relationship management, professional reconciliation, comprehensive audit tracking, and an intuitive user experience while preserving all existing functionality and introducing **zero functional regressions**.
