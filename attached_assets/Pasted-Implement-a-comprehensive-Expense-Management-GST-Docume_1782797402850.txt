Implement a comprehensive **Expense Management, GST Document Management, and Financial Control** module designed for business owners, accountants, and finance teams. The module should provide a simple, transparent, and enterprise-grade workflow for managing all company expenses, project costs, GST documents, and financial records.

### Expense Management

* Record all business expenses including Office Rent, Electricity (EB), Internet, Fuel, Travel, Food, Office Supplies, Software Subscriptions, Salaries, Repairs, Asset Purchases, Marketing, Maintenance, Project Expenses, Vendor Payments, Petty Cash, and custom expense categories.
* Support project-wise, department-wise, branch-wise, cost-center-wise, employee-wise, and client-wise expense tracking.
* Allow employees to submit expenses with receipts for approval.
* Multi-level approval workflow for expense claims.
* Track Paid, Pending, Approved, Rejected, Reimbursed, and Outstanding expenses.
* Maintain complete audit trails and expense history.

### GST & Document Repository

* Upload GST invoices, bills, receipts, purchase documents, vendor invoices, and supporting documents.
* Organize documents by Financial Year, Month (e.g., June 2026), GST Period, Vendor, Project, Expense Category, or Department.
* Support drag-and-drop uploads, bulk uploads, document preview, OCR-ready storage, version history, tagging, and powerful search.
* Link uploaded documents directly to purchases, expenses, invoices, payments, journal entries, and accounting transactions.
* Maintain a centralized, searchable GST document repository for audits and compliance.

### Financial Tracking

* Track all money received from customers and all company expenditures in real time.
* Display clear summaries of:

  * Total Revenue
  * Total Expenses
  * Gross Profit
  * Net Profit
  * Outstanding Receivables
  * Outstanding Payables
  * Available Cash
  * Bank Balance
  * GST Payable/Receivable
  * Project Profitability
  * Department-wise Expenses
  * Budget vs Actual Spending
* Provide complete cash flow visibility with drill-down reports.

### Auditor Collaboration

* Generate auditor-ready document packages by Financial Year, GST period, project, customer, or vendor.
* Export selected records and attachments as a ZIP package or PDF bundle.
* Email reports and supporting documents directly to auditors from the application with secure attachments.
* Maintain document sharing history and audit logs.

### Reports & Analytics

* Expense Reports
* GST Purchase Register
* GST Sales Register
* Expense vs Income Analysis
* Cash Flow Statement
* Project Cost Reports
* Department Cost Reports
* Profit & Loss
* Vendor Payment Reports
* Customer Collection Reports
* Budget Variance Reports
* Financial Dashboards with interactive charts and KPIs.

### User Experience

* Modern enterprise-grade UI/UX with intuitive dashboards, timelines, monthly document views, advanced search, filters, tags, pagination, quick actions, drag-and-drop uploads, responsive layouts, and seamless navigation.
* Enable users to locate, validate, review, export, or share financial records within seconds.

The module should provide complete financial transparency, making it easy to understand how much money has been received, how much has been spent, what is due, what is outstanding, available balances, GST liabilities, project profitability, and overall business performance while simplifying GST filing, auditing, compliance, and financial reporting.
