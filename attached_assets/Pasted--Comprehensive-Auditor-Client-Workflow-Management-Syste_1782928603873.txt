# Comprehensive Auditor & Client Workflow Management System (God Mode)

Build a complete, enterprise-grade **Audit Practice Management System** that supports the day-to-day operations of Chartered Accountants, Auditors, Tax Consultants, and Accounting Firms managing multiple clients.

The system should allow an audit firm to manage hundreds of clients simultaneously, where each client has independent workflows, compliance requirements, statutory filings, document requests, pending tasks, approvals, reminders, and communication history.

## Core Objective

Design a centralized platform where an Auditor can create, assign, track, monitor, approve, and complete every compliance-related activity for each client from a single dashboard.

The application should support end-to-end audit lifecycle management with complete visibility, accountability, and document tracking.

# Final Objective

Develop a complete **Audit Practice Management Platform** where auditors can efficiently manage multiple clients, assign any type of compliance or document request, monitor progress, automate reminders, track approvals, and maintain a complete audit trail from a single system. The platform should provide a seamless collaboration experience between the audit firm and its clients, ensuring every compliance activity, document request, follow-up, approval, and statutory filing is tracked end-to-end. The solution should be scalable, highly configurable, enterprise-grade, and ready for real-world production use without disrupting existing business logic or workflows.
